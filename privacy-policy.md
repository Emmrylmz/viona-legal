# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 27, 2024**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Viona ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name (first and last name)
- Date of birth
- Gender (optional)
- Email address
- Phone number

### Device Information
- Device model and operating system
- Device identifiers for push notifications
- App version

### Location Data
- Your location (with your permission) to show nearby venues

### Usage Data
- Reservation history and preferences
- App interaction data
- Crash reports and diagnostics

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Process and manage your reservations
- Send booking confirmations, reminders, and updates
- Show nearby barber shops and venues
- Improve our app and services
- Send promotional communications (with your consent)

## Data Sharing

We may share your information with:
- **Service Providers**: Firebase (authentication), Sentry (crash reporting), Google (sign-in)
- **Venues**: Your name and contact info when you make a reservation
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure token-based authentication
- Encrypted storage for sensitive data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Opt-out of promotional communications
- Withdraw consent for location access

## Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. You can request deletion by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
- Email: support@viona.app
