# Account Deletion Request

**Last Updated: December 27, 2024**

## How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Viona account and all associated data using one of the following methods:

### Option 1: In-App Deletion
1. Open the Viona app
2. Go to Settings
3. Select "Account"
4. Tap "Delete Account"
5. Confirm your request

### Option 2: Email Request
Send an email to **support@viona.app** with:
- Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
- Your registered email address or phone number
- Confirmation that you want to delete your account

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, we will permanently remove:
- Your profile information (name, date of birth, gender)
- Your email address and phone number
- Your reservation history
- Your preferences and settings
- Your device tokens and notification preferences

## What Happens After Deletion

- Your account will be deactivated immediately
- All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
- This action cannot be undone
- You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Viona again

## Data Retention Exceptions

Some data may be retained for legal or business purposes:
- Transaction records (as required by law)
- Data necessary to resolve disputes
- Anonymized, aggregated data for analytics

## Processing Time

- In-app deletion: Immediate
- Email requests: Within 7 business days

## Contact Us

If you have questions about account deletion, contact us at:
- Email: support@viona.app
